VENOM 2

Venom 2 is an upcoming American superhero film based on the Marvel Comics character
Venom is produced by Columbia Pictures in association with Marvel
Distributed by Sony Pictures Releasing, it is intended to be the third film in Sony's Marvel Universe and the sequel to Venom (2018).
The film is being directed by Andy Serkis from a screenplay by Kelly Marcel, and stars Tom Hardy as Eddie Brock / Venom, alongside Woody Harrelson, Michelle Williams, Reid Scott, and Naomie Harris.
